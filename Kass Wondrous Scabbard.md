## Kass Wondrous Scabbard
_Wondrous Item (Container), Rare_

### Description
The scabbard has three reinforced compartments connected to an extradimensional space. The shortest compartment is designed to sheath up to 6 daggers, or 4 one-handed simple melee weapons. The midsize compartment is designed to hold up to 3 versatile weapons (spears, quarterstaffs, longswords). The largest compartment holds up to 2 larger objects, such as those with the two-handed or heavy property, excluding a long bow. The scabbard is incapable of holding short or long bows, crossbows, or weapons with a Special property.

Placing the Scabbard inside an extradimensional space created by a bag of holding, portable hole, or similar item instantly destroys both items and opens a gate to the Astral Plane. The gate originates where the one item was placed inside the other. Any creature within 10 feet of the gate is sucked through it and deposited in a random location on the Astral Plane. The gate then closes. The gate is one-way only and can't be reopened.
 
You can draw or stow any item the Scabbard contains as if doing so from a regular or scabbard.
